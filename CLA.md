## 1. When this Agreement applies

1.1 This Agreement applies to **substantive contributions** to Nyuchi's
**Apache 2.0 infrastructure repositories** — for example
[`ntl`](https://github.com/nyuchi/ntl),
[`siafudb`](https://github.com/nyuchi/siafudb),
[`siafudb-kuzu`](https://github.com/nyuchi/siafudb-kuzu), the Honeycomb
protocol, and similar primitives — as required by NA-02 §4(b).

1.2 Contributions to other Nyuchi repositories require, at minimum, the
Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) sign-off described in
[`CONTRIBUTING.md`](https://github.com/nyuchi/.github/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
Where a repository's own `CONTRIBUTING` file requires this CLA, that
requirement controls for that repository.

1.3 This Agreement is executed **once per contributor** and applies to
**all your past and future contributions** to the repositories in scope,
unless and until you revoke it for future contributions under §7.

## 10. How to sign

You sign this Agreement **electronically, once**, before your first
in-scope Contribution is merged:

- **Individuals** — when you open your first pull request to an in-scope
  repository, the CLA assistant comments with a link; agreeing there (or
  replying with the statement it provides) records your acceptance
  against your GitHub identity. Your Git `Signed-off-by` email must match
  the identity you sign with.
- **Entities (corporate CLA)** — an authorised signatory emails
  [legal@nyuchi.com](mailto:legal@nyuchi.com) to execute a corporate CLA
  and to maintain the list of employees authorised to contribute under
  it.

Records of who has signed are maintained by Nyuchi. Questions about this
Agreement go to [legal@nyuchi.com](mailto:legal@nyuchi.com).
